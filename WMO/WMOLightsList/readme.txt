  WMOInformation.exe
    Prints information from some chunks in the WMO.

    Usage: "WMOInformation.exe <WMOFile>"

  WMOLightsList.exe
    Prints a list with all lights and colors of the WMO.

    Usage: "WMOLightsList.exe <WMOFile>"

  WMOSetLightsToColor.exe
    Sets all lights in a WMO to a specific color.

    Usage: "WMOSetLightsToColor.exe <WMO File> <col (0xff000000 | 0xXXRRGGBB)>"
    Built at: Oct 12 2009
